Intro

Towards the end of 2021, Galway film centre was looking to rebrand. Alongside this rebrand was the design of a new website. Under the creative direction of Diarmuid Slattery and Dave Garvin at New Graphic, I worked on the visual design of a new website for the new brand Ardán. The website was designed to be flexable and fast to build to launch the new brand without compromising quality and visual design.
