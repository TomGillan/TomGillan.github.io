Motyf is a moving typography festival coming to dun laoraighe, the following identity comprises of a unique, motion based typeface and colour scheme for the festival, and celebrated diversity and foregrounds the creative minds attending the event.

In Collaberation with David pakenham a suitable digital brand was developed using different geometric forms to create the letter M, symbolic of the symposium. This project was extended across print, screen , titles and web.


Client: Motyf
Collaborators: David Pakenham

Alongside the creation of the motyf mark there wher aslso produced a set of abstract compositions that could be used throughout the website and inbetween speakers at the symposium, extending the original visual language and boosting it across media.
