Motion design

Motyf was a moving typography festival hosted in 2018, in the Dun Laoghaire Lexicon. In collaboration with David Pakenham, a suitable identity was developed using different geometric forms to create the letter M, symbolic of the symposium. This project was extended across print, screen, titles and web.Â 

Alongside the creation of the Motyf mark, there was also a set of abstract compositions produced that could be used throughout the website and in between speakers at the symposium, extending the original visual language and boosting it across media.
