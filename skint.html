Skint is a film about a young teenage girl from Cabra, who discovers that she is pregnant. The design solution focuses on the pregnancy, and the general theme is that this has resulted in her life as she knows it, falling apart. The visual style is neutral to suit the neutral stance that the film takes, as it objectively presents the prospect of unwanted pregnancy.


Client: IADT Film school
Director: Tara O'Callaghan
