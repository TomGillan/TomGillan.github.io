Role

Intro

Motyf was a moving typography festival hosted in 2018 in the Dun Laoghaire Lexicon. In collaboration with David Pakenham, a suitable identity was developed using different geometric forms to create the symposium's symbolic letter M. Assets that I created were used across print, screen, titles and web.
