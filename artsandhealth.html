Role

Intro

Arts and Health work with medical organisations to improve the quality of life and cultural access in healthcare settings.

The website is a space to capture the great work and insights within the crossover of art and healthcare, and offers guidance for artists, creatives, and wellbeing facilitators to work with patients in care settings.

Alongside the website, I also updated the organisation's logo, ensuring it was accessible and matched its new ambitions. All work was completed at New Graphic.
