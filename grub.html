To make insects appealing for consumption to our audience by putting the image of insects in the background and instead forefronting the flavours and eco/nutri benefits. More specificallythw audiece was targeted by highlighting the familiar flavours to eleviate the foreignness of eating bugs for mainly European and American people.

App walkthrough

the bugs where abstracted by buiding them from geometric forms, which were then used throughout the visual language. Using these same forms in both the bugs, logo and other imagery,showing the idea that everything is linked.

The app facilitates levels of use, where people using the app to begin with are presented with the bug deconstructed, and the product in it’s most processed form.

As the user gets more comfortable with eating insects, their level increases and they are presented with insects in more whole form.

The colour palate refelcts primarlily to the flavours shown on our flavour spetrum, and the packaging colours refer to the flavour of that bug. Our posters create intrugue while refelcting the tone of voice of our app.
