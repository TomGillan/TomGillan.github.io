Banished is an Opera put on by the Royal Irish Academy of Music. Banished tells the story of a group of women deported to Australia aboard an 1800s prison ship. The typographic design was used to position the opera as more of a cultural event, than commercial. Throughout the opera the women struggle against oppression, and their personalities struggle to break free from the ropes of their captivity. Motion design was used to genrate variable posters, cellebrating the opera's energetic and diverse charecters.
