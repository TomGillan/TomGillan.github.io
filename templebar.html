Website visual design

Designed at

Pixel Design

Creative direction

Karen Hanratty

While working at Pixel Design worked with and under the creative direction of Karen Hanratty to work on the UI of a new website for Temple Bar Gallery + Studios. The design was kept simple and stripped back, alowing for the artwork of the Gallery to take centre fold. Whitespace and typography played an important role in the look and feel of the site.
