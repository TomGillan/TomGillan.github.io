Mix-n-match

An event to bring together music and motion to promote creativity and kickstart collaboration. Selected work will be exhibited on digital screens around Dublin for people to mix with music to create matches and celebrate the unexpected.

Match music with motion

New music mixes will be released weekly for you to match with 30 of the best motion submissions which will be exhibited around Dublin on 4k digital screens.

Follow these 4 steps to create your distinct match and have your match featured at the mix-n-match launch.

Why

Mix and match was founded to channel the public’s creative spirit to bring beautiful music and motion together and to kickstart new collaboration between creatives.

The main challenge we encounter is that the creative work shared on digital platforms is usually only experienced by other designers.

Mix-n-match is an event to bring music and motion together to create unexpected collaborations, showcase the cities creativity and provide beautiful motion graphics for public enjoyment.
