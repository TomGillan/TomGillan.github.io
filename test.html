Mix-n-match

An event to bring together music and motion to promote creativity and kickstart collaboration. Selected work will be exhibited on digital screens around Dublin for people to mix with music to create matches and celebrate the unexpected.

An event to bring together music and motion to promote creativity and kickstart collaboration. Selected work will be exhibited on digital screens around Dublin for people to mix with music to create matches and celebrate the unexpected.

Match music
with motion

New music mixes will be released weekly for you to match with 30 of the best motion submissions which will be exhibited around Dublin on 4k digital screens.

Follow these 4 steps to create your distinct match and have your match featured at the mix-n-match launch.
