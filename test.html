Lookit

An event to bring together music and motion to promote creativity and kickstart collaboration. Selected work will be exhibited on digital screens around Dublin for people to mix with music to create matches and celebrate the unexpected.

We will display kinetics on digital screens in 30 locations around Dublin during the Offset weekend. 2020.04.03–05 18:00—00:00

kinetic poster
×

Put simply, a kinetic is an animated poster.

But unlike a traditional animation a kinetic has to behave like a traditional printed poster. Meaning it needs to convey an idea or message in the time it takes to walk or flick past. It must engage and entice, but not confuse the audience, or at least not to the point where they forget the original idea or message.

Typically there is one scene, no jump scare tactics, nothing that might cause epilepsy or any other types of accidents.
